<p><ul><li>Silicon Photovoltaic</li><li>CIGS Thin Film Photovoltaic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Solar Shingle Market includes two main types of technologies: Silicon Photovoltaic and CIGS Thin Film Photovoltaic. Silicon Photovoltaic shingles are made from silicon wafers and are the most common type of solar shingle. They are known for their high efficiency and durability. On the other hand, CIGS Thin Film Photovoltaic shingles are made from a combination of copper, indium, gallium, and selenide. They are thinner and lighter than silicon shingles, making them easier to install on various roof types.</p></p>
