<p><p>Gasoline engine management systems market includes various components such as Engine Electronic Control Units (ECUs), Electric Fuel Pumps, Air Mass Flowmeters, Ignition Coils, Oxygen Sensors, and others. Air mass flowmeters measure the amount of air entering the engine. Ignition coils generate high-voltage electricity for spark plugs. Oxygen sensors monitor the oxygen levels in the exhaust to optimize fuel efficiency. Other components assist in overall engine performance and emissions control.</p></p>
