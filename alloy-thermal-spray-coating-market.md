<p><p>Alloy Thermal Spray Coating markets can be categorized into two main types: Combustion Flame Process and Electrical Process. The Combustion Flame Process involves the use of a high-velocity flame to melt and propel the coating material onto a surface, while the Electrical Process involves the use of an electrical arc or plasma to melt and propel the coating material. Both processes are widely used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing for their ability to provide corrosion resistance, wear protection, and thermal insulation.</p></p>
