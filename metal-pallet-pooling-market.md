<p><p>Metal pallet pooling involves the sharing and reuse of metal pallets within a supply chain. There are two main market types within metal pallet pooling: pallet rental and pallet pooling. In pallet rental, companies pay a fee to rent metal pallets for a specific period of time. In pallet pooling, companies contribute and exchange metal pallets within a shared network, allowing for increased efficiency and reduced costs in handling and transporting goods. Both market types offer sustainable and cost-effective solutions for businesses looking to optimize their supply chain operations.</p></p>
