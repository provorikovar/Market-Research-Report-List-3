<p><p>Smectite clays are categorized into two main types: sodium bentonite and calcium bentonite. Sodium bentonite is mainly used for its swelling properties, making it ideal for applications such as sealing ponds and landfills. On the other hand, calcium bentonite is known for its high absorbency, making it suitable for uses such as litter and drilling mud. Both types of smectite clays play important roles in various industries due to their unique properties and characteristics.</p></p>
