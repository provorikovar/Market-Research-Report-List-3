<p><p>Thermal conductive grease is used to improve heat transfer between electronic components and heat sinks. Silicone-based grease is the most common type, offering good thermal conductivity and electrical insulation. On the other hand, non-silicone-based grease, such as graphite or zinc oxide-based, are preferred in applications where silicone contamination is a concern. Both types provide efficient thermal management, but silicone-based grease is more versatile, while non-silicone-based grease offers specific advantages for certain applications. Both types cater to the diverse needs of the thermal conductive grease market.</p></p>
