<p><p>District heating pipe market can be categorized into two main types: hot water heating network and steam heating network. Hot water heating network involves circulating hot water through pipes to provide heat to buildings and homes. On the other hand, steam heating network utilizes steam as the primary source of heat distribution. Both types of district heating networks offer efficient and sustainable solutions for heating large areas, leading to reduced energy consumption and greenhouse gas emissions.</p></p>
