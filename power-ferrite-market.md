MnZn Power Ferrite is composed of manganese-zinc and is commonly used in power conversion devices and electromagnetic interference suppression. On the other hand, NiZn Power Ferrite contains nickel-zinc and is often used in high-frequency transformers and inductors. Both types of Power Ferrite offer high saturation magnetization and low eddy current losses, making them ideal for use in power electronics and telecommunications industries.</p></p>
