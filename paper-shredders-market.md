<p><p>Paper shredders come in various types to meet different shredding needs. Strip-cut shredders cut paper into long strips, providing basic security for everyday shredding. Cross-cut shredders provide higher security by cutting paper into small pieces. Particle-cut shredders cut paper into tiny confetti-like particles, offering the highest level of security. Cardboard shredders are specifically designed to shred cardboard boxes and packaging materials. Each type of shredder caters to specific requirements, providing different levels of security and efficiency.</p></p>
