<p><p>The Vehicle Intelligent Headlight market includes different types of advanced lighting systems such as Adaptive Front-lighting System (AFS) and Adaptive Driving Beam System (ADB). AFS adjusts the direction and intensity of the headlights based on the vehicle's speed and steering input to improve visibility around corners. ADB further enhances safety by automatically switching between high and low beams to avoid glaring oncoming drivers while maintaining optimal illumination of the road ahead. Both technologies contribute to improved driver visibility and road safety.</p></p>
