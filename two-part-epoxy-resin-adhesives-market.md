<p><p>Two-part epoxy resin adhesives come in two main types: rigid and flexible. Rigid epoxy resin adhesives are strong and durable, making them ideal for bonding materials that require stiffness and rigidity. This type of adhesive is commonly used in construction, automotive, and aerospace industries. On the other hand, flexible epoxy resin adhesives have a certain degree of flexibility, allowing them to bond materials that undergo frequent movement or stress. This type of adhesive is often used in electronics, marine, and medical applications.</p></p>
