<p><p>Antilock braking system modulators come in various types based on the number of channels they have. One-channel ABS modulators control the brakes on one wheel, while two-channel ABS modulators control brakes on two wheels. Three-channel ABS modulators are responsible for three wheels, while four-channel ABS modulators control all four wheels independently. Each type offers different levels of control and effectiveness in preventing wheel lock-up during braking, enhancing overall vehicle safety and control.</p></p>
