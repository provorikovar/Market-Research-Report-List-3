<p><p>The prefabricated home market consists of two main types: permanent and relocatable homes. Permanent prefabricated homes are designed to be assembled on a permanent foundation and remain in one location for an extended period of time. On the other hand, relocatable prefabricated homes are designed to be easily dismantled, moved, and reassembled in different locations as needed. Both types offer the convenience and cost-efficiency of prefabrication, with the main difference being their intended use and longevity.</p></p>
