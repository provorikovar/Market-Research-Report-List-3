<p><ul><li>Shell and Tube</li><li>Plate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Titanium heat exchangers are commonly found in two main types - shell and tube, and plate heat exchangers. </p><p>Shell and tube heat exchangers consist of a shell with multiple tubes running through it, allowing for efficient heat transfer between two fluids. These types are typically used for high-pressure applications or where a large heating or cooling surface is needed.</p><p>Plate heat exchangers are compact and efficient, consisting of multiple plates stacked together. They are commonly used in HVAC systems or in industries where space-saving solutions are required.</p></p>
