<p><strong>The Mineral Castings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Epoxy Mineral Casting</li><li>Polymer Minreal Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mineral castings are commonly categorized into two main types: epoxy mineral casting and polymer mineral casting. Epoxy mineral casting involves a process where epoxy resin is mixed with mineral compounds to create a strong and durable material ideal for industrial applications. On the other hand, polymer mineral casting utilizes polymer resin instead of epoxy, providing similar properties but with variations in terms of strength and flexibility. Both types offer high precision and excellent thermal stability, making them popular choices in various industries.</p></p>
