<p><p>Metallic gaskets and seals come in various types, including spiral wound, metal jacketed, and others. Spiral wound gaskets are made by winding a metal strip and filler material in a circular shape, providing high strength and elasticity. Metal jacketed gaskets have a metal outer shell and filler material inside, offering excellent sealing performance. Other types of metallic gaskets include Kammprofile, corrugated, and solid metal gaskets, each catering to different sealing requirements in industrial applications.</p></p>
