<p><p>Disinfectant chemicals market can be categorized into various types including formalin, glutaraldehyde, hydrogen peroxide, calcium hypochlorites, and chloramines. Calcium hypochlorites are powerful oxidizing agents with disinfecting properties. Chloramines are a combination of chlorine and ammonia used for disinfection. Each type of disinfectant chemical offers unique properties and applications in the market.</p></p>
