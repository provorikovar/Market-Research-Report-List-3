<p><strong>The Yarn Lubricant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spin Finish</li><li>Coning Oils</li><li>Knitting Oils</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Yarn lubricants are used in the textile industry to improve the performance of yarn during processing. Spin finish lubricants are applied during spinning to reduce friction and static electricity. Coning oils are used during winding to prevent yarn from sticking together. Knitting oils are applied during knitting to reduce yarn breakage and improve stitch formation. Each type of lubricant serves a specific purpose in the yarn manufacturing process, helping to enhance the quality and efficiency of production.</p></p>
