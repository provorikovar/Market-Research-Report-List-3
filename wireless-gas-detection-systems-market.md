<p><p>Wireless gas detection systems are available in two main types, stationary and portable. Stationary systems are fixed in a particular location and continuously monitor the surrounding area for gas leaks or hazards. These are commonly used in industrial settings such as manufacturing plants or refineries. Portable systems, on the other hand, are designed to be easily carried from one location to another for on-the-go gas detection. These are often used by field technicians, emergency responders, or for spot-checking gas levels in various environments.</p></p>
