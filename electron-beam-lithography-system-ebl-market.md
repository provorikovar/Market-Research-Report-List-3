<p><p>Electron Beam Lithography System (EBL) is a powerful technology used in the fabrication of nanoscale structures. Two main types of EBL systems are Gaussian beam and Shaped beam. Gaussian beam EBL systems use a simple round beam shape for patterning, suitable for basic lithography applications. On the other hand, Shaped beam EBL systems utilize advanced beam shaping techniques to achieve complex and precise patterning, making them ideal for cutting-edge research and development in nanotechnology. Both types of EBL systems play important roles in enabling the production of advanced semiconductor devices and other nanoscale technologies.</p></p>
