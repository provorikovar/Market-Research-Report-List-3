<p><p>Raman spectroscopy is used in various modes in different markets. Immersion mode involves inserting the sample into a liquid medium to enhance signal quality, making it ideal for biochemical and pharmaceutical industries. Stand-off mode, on the other hand, allows for non-contact analysis at a distance, making it suitable for security and defense applications where direct sample handling is not feasible. Both modes cater to specific market needs by providing versatile and efficient solutions for spectral analysis.</p></p>
