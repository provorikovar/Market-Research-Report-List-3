<p><p>Sound attenuators are devices designed to reduce noise levels in ventilation and air conditioning systems. Rectangular silencers are the most common type, typically used in ductwork to absorb and dissipate sound waves. Elbow silencers are specifically designed for use in ductwork elbows, while circular silencers are used in round ducts. Other types of sound attenuators include acoustic louvers, splitter silencers, and discharge attenuators. Each type of attenuator serves a unique purpose in reducing noise levels in various HVAC systems.</p></p>
