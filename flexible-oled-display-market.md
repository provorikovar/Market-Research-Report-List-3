<p><strong>The Flexible OLED Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>Flexible OLED Display market is categorized into two main types - AMOLED Display and PMOLED Display. </p><p>AMOLED Display, or Active Matrix OLED Display, is a type of OLED display technology that uses a TFT backplane to control the flow of current to each individual pixel. This allows for faster response times and higher resolution displays.</p><p>PMOLED Display, or Passive Matrix OLED Display, is a simpler version of OLED technology that uses a grid of electrodes to control the flow of current to the pixels. This results in lower resolution displays and slower response times compared to AMOLED displays.</p></p>
