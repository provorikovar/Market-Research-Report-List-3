<p><p>Display bonding adhesives are used in electronic devices to bond display screens to the device body. Optically clear adhesive (OCA) is a type of adhesive that is transparent, allowing for high visibility of the display. Liquid optical clear adhesive (LOCA) is a type of OCA that is applied as a liquid before curing into a solid, providing a stronger bond. Both types are essential in the display bonding adhesive market for providing clarity and durability to electronic devices.</p></p>
