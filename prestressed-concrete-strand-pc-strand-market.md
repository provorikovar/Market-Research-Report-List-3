<p><p>Prestressed Concrete Strand (PC Strand) is a type of high-strength, pre-stressed steel strand typically used in the construction industry for reinforcing concrete structures. The three main types of PC Strand are Bare PC Strand, which is untreated and exposed, Grease Filled PC Strand, which is coated with grease for corrosion protection, and Wax Filled PC Strand, which is coated with wax for enhanced durability. Each type of PC Strand offers unique benefits and is used in various construction applications based on specific project requirements.</p></p>
