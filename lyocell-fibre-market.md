<p><ul><li>Regular Lyocell Fiber</li><li>Crosslinked Lyocell Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Regular Lyocell Fiber is a type of Lyocell fiber that is produced using a solvent spinning process, resulting in a smooth and soft cellulose fiber that is highly absorbent and breathable. On the other hand, Crosslinked Lyocell Fiber undergoes a crosslinking process that enhances its strength and durability, making it suitable for applications where extra durability is required. Both types of Lyocell fibers are eco-friendly and sustainable alternatives to traditional synthetic fibers, offering a combination of comfort, performance, and environmental benefits.</p></p>
