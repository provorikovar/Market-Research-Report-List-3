<p><p>Supervisory circuits are essential components in electronic devices to monitor the power supply voltage and protect the system from voltage fluctuations. There are two main types of supervisory circuits based on their packaging- SMD (surface-mounted device) type and Through Hole type. SMD type circuits are compact and easy to integrate onto PCBs, while Through Hole type circuits are larger and more robust, suitable for heavy-duty applications. Both types offer reliable voltage monitoring and reset functions, making them widely used in various industries.</p></p>
