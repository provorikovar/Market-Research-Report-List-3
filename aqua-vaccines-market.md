<p><p>Aqua vaccines market consists of three main types: Injectable vaccines, Dip vaccines, and Oral vaccines. Injectable vaccines are administered through injection for individual fish, providing protection against diseases. Dip vaccines are used by immersing fish in a solution to deliver immunity, usually during handling or transportation. Oral vaccines are consumed by fish through their food or water, offering a convenient method of vaccination. Each type of vaccine serves a specific purpose in the prevention of diseases in aquaculture.</p></p>
