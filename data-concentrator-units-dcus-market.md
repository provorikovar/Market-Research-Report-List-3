<p><p>Data Concentrator Units (DCUs) are devices used to collect and process data from different sources. They come in two main types: Wired Type and Wireless Type. Wired DCUs are connected to devices through physical cables, providing a reliable and stable connection. On the other hand, Wireless DCUs use radio frequency signals to communicate with devices, offering more flexibility and mobility. Both types cater to different market needs, with Wired Type being suitable for fixed installations and Wireless Type being ideal for applications requiring mobility and flexibility.</p></p>
