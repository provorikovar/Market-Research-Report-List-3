<p><ul><li>Multimode Fibre</li><li>Single-mode Fibre</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Multimode fibre optics is commonly used in short-distance applications due to its larger core size, which allows multiple signals to travel simultaneously. It is cost-effective and suits applications such as local area networks. Single-mode fibre optics, on the other hand, has a smaller core size and is ideal for long-distance transmissions as it minimizes signal loss. It is commonly used in telecommunications and data centers where high-speed and high-bandwidth requirements are needed. Both multimode and single-mode fibres cater to different market needs based on their specific characteristics.</p></p>
