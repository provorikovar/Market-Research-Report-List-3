<p><p>Electro-Static Discharge (ESD) tape is commonly available in two types - Single Side Tape and Double Sides Tape. Single Side Tape is adhesive on one side only, used for securing components in place and providing ESD protection. Double Sides Tape has adhesive on both sides, ideal for applications where two surfaces need to be bonded together securely and conductive properties are required to prevent ESD. Both types are essential tools in electronics manufacturing and assembly processes to protect sensitive components from damaging electro-static discharge.</p></p>
