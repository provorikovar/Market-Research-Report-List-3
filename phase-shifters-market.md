<p><p>Phase shifters are devices that can change the phase of an input signal without altering its amplitude. They are commonly used in various applications like radar systems, communication systems, and phased array antennas. There are two main types of phase shifters in the market: analog type and digital type. Analog phase shifters use variable capacitors or inductors to adjust the phase of the signal continuously, while digital phase shifters use digital circuitry to change the phase in discrete steps. Both types have their own advantages and are suitable for different applications.</p></p>
