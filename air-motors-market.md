<p><ul><li>Vane Air Motor</li><li>Piston Air Motor</li><li>Gear Air Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air motors are classified into three main types - Vane Air Motor, Piston Air Motor, and Gear Air Motor. Vane air motors operate by utilizing rotating vanes to create mechanical energy. Piston air motors use a reciprocating piston to generate power. Gear air motors use a system of gears to convert compressed air into rotational motion. Each type of air motor has its own advantages and applications, making them suitable for various industries such as automotive, manufacturing, and aerospace.</p></p>
