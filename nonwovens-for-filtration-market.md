<p><p>Nonwovens for Filtration market includes various types such as Airlaid, Wetlaid, Spunlaid, Meltblown, Needlepunching, Hydro-entanglement, and Thermobonded. Airlaid nonwovens are made by air-laying fibers into a web, Wetlaid uses water to form the web, Spunlaid involves spinning fibers onto a conveyor belt, Meltblown uses high-velocity air to blow melted fibers onto a collector, Needlepunching mechanically punches fibers together, Hydro-entanglement uses water jets to form the web, and Thermobonded involves using heat to bond fibers together.</p></p>
