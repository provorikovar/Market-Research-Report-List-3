<p><p>Surface protection tapes are classified into different types based on the material they are made of. The PE (polyethylene) tapes offer flexibility and strong adhesive properties. PET (polyethylene terephthalate) tapes provide excellent abrasion and heat resistance. PP (polypropylene) tapes are known for their high tensile strength and tear resistance. PU (polyurethane) tapes offer superior durability and chemical resistance. PVC (polyvinyl chloride) tapes are popular for their affordability and weather resistance. Each type of tape caters to specific needs in industries such as automotive, construction, and electronics.</p></p>
