<p><ul><li>Spray Drying</li><li>Freeze Drying</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Instant black coffee is available in two main types in the market - spray drying and freeze drying. Spray drying involves the process of spraying hot water extract of coffee into a stream of hot air, resulting in instant coffee powder. On the other hand, freeze drying involves freezing the coffee extract and then removing the ice through sublimation, resulting in a more delicate and flavorful instant coffee. Both types have their unique advantages and are popular choices among consumers for their convenience and quick preparation.</p></p>
