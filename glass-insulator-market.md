<p><p>The glass insulator market consists of two main types: Suspension Glass Insulators and Pin Glass Insulators. Suspension Glass Insulators are typically used in high voltage transmission lines and are designed to support the conductor cable. Pin Glass Insulators are used in distribution systems and are mounted on cross arms to support the conductors. Both types of insulators play a crucial role in preventing electrical current from leaking to the ground and ensuring the efficient transmission of electricity.</p></p>
