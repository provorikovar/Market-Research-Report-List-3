<p><p>Implantable Cardiac Defibrillator (ICD) Market includes two main types - Single Chamber and Dual Chamber devices. Single Chamber ICDs have one lead connected to the right ventricle, while Dual Chamber ICDs have leads connected to both the right ventricle and the right atrium. Single Chamber ICDs are simpler in design and usually more cost-effective, while Dual Chamber ICDs offer more advanced monitoring and pacing capabilities. Both types are used to treat abnormal heart rhythms and prevent sudden cardiac arrest.</p></p>
