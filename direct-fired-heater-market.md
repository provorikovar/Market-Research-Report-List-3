<p><p>Direct Fired Heater market includes three main types: Radiant Heater, Convection Heater, and Radiant-Convection Heater. Radiant Heater emits heat directly from its surface, ideal for spot heating. Convection Heater distributes heat through the air, heating the entire space evenly. Radiant-Convection Heater combines both methods for efficient heating. Each type caters to different heating needs, offering flexibility and effectiveness in various environments. It is essential for consumers to consider the specific heating requirements before investing in any type of Direct Fired Heater.</p></p>
