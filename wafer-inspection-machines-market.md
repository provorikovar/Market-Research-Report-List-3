<p><ul><li>Optical Wafer Inspection</li><li>E-beam Wafer Inspection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wafer inspection machines are used in the semiconductor industry to detect defects in wafers during the manufacturing process. Optical wafer inspection machines use light to detect defects on wafers, while e-beam wafer inspection machines use electron beams for the same purpose. Optical wafer inspection machines are more commonly used due to their lower cost and faster inspection speed, while e-beam wafer inspection machines offer higher resolution and are better suited for detecting smaller defects. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the quality of semiconductor wafers.</p></p>
