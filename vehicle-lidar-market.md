<p><p>Vehicle LiDAR technology can be categorized into three main types: Mechanical LiDAR, Solid State LiDAR, and Hybrid Solid-state LiDAR. </p><p>Mechanical LiDAR systems use rotating mirrors to scan the environment, while Solid State LiDAR systems utilize fixed components such as semiconductor lasers. Hybrid Solid-state LiDAR systems combine elements of both Mechanical and Solid State LiDAR to provide high-resolution mapping capabilities. Each type of LiDAR technology has its own advantages and applications in the automotive industry for autonomous driving and advanced driver assistance systems.</p></p>
