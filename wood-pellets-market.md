<p><p>Wood pellets market is divided into two main types based on color: white pellets and black pellets. White pellets are made from sawdust and other wood residues, offering higher energy efficiency and cleaner combustion. On the other hand, black pellets are produced through torrefaction, a process that increases their energy density and water resistance. While white pellets are more common in the market and preferred for residential heating, black pellets are gaining popularity in industrial applications due to their higher energy content.</p></p>
