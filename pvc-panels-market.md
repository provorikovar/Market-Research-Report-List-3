<p><p>PVC panels are available in three main market types: cellular, rigid, and hollow. Cellular PVC panels have a foam core structure, making them lightweight and easy to install. Rigid PVC panels are solid throughout, providing strength and durability for various applications. Hollow PVC panels have a hollow core structure, making them lighter and more cost-effective. Each market type offers unique benefits and features, catering to different needs and preferences in the construction and design industry.</p></p>
