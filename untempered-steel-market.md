<p><strong>The Untempered Steel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ferrite-pearlite Steel</li><li>Bainite Steel</li><li>Martensitic Steel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Untempered steel markets can be segmented into three main types based on the microstructure of the steel: ferrite-pearlite steel, bainite steel, and martensitic steel. Ferrite-pearlite steel consists of a mix of ferrite and pearlite phases, offering a balance of strength and ductility. Bainite steel is characterized by a fine microstructure that provides high strength and toughness. Martensitic steel is known for its high hardness and wear resistance. Each type of untempered steel offers unique properties suitable for different applications in various industries.</p></p>
