<p><p>Dye medical laser market types include liquid dye and solid-state dye. Liquid dye lasers use a liquid solution as the gain medium, offering tunability and high power output. Solid-state dye lasers use solid-state materials, such as crystals or glasses, as the gain medium, providing better stability and durability. Both types of dye lasers are widely used in medical applications such as dermatology, ophthalmology, and surgery for procedures like skin rejuvenation, tattoo removal, and eye surgeries.</p></p>
