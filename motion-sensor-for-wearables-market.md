<p><p>Motion sensors for wearables such as accelerometers and gyroscopes are essential components that enable various features in devices like smartwatches and fitness trackers. Accelerometers track the acceleration of the wearer's movements in multiple directions, allowing for features like step counting and gesture control. Gyroscopes, on the other hand, measure the device's orientation and rotation, enabling features like screen rotation and augmented reality applications. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in enhancing the user experience and functionality of wearable devices.</p></p>
