<p><p>Dental articulating paper comes in two main types: horseshoe and straight strip. Horseshoe articulating paper is shaped like a half-moon to fit around the teeth, providing a more precise marking of occlusal contact points. Straight strip articulating paper is rectangular and can be easily cut to the desired length, making it versatile for use on various teeth. Both types of articulating paper help dentists identify high points of contact between the teeth and adjust the bite for proper alignment.</p></p>
