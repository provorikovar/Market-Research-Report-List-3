<p><p>Industrial Oil Skimmers market comprises of various types like Belt Oil Skimmers, Disk Oil Skimmers, and Others. Belt Oil Skimmers are designed to remove oil from the surface of water or coolant using a continuous belt that runs through the fluid. Disk Oil Skimmers use a rotating disk to attract oil, which is then scraped off and collected. Other types of oil skimmers may include tube, brush, or mop skimmers, each with their own unique mechanism for oil removal in industrial settings.</p></p>
