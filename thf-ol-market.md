<p><p>THF-OL (tetrahydrofurfuryl alcohol) is a versatile compound used in various industries. The market for THF-OL is segmented into three types: R-3-THF-OL, S-3-THF-OL, and Rac-3-THF-OL. R-3-THF-OL is the optically active form, S-3-THF-OL is the mirror image of R-3-THF-OL, and Rac-3-THF-OL is the racemic mixture of both forms.
