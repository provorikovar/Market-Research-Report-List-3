<p><ul><li>Film Laminates</li><li>Reinforced Laminates</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aircraft laminates market includes both film laminates and reinforced laminates. Film laminates are thin layers of film applied to aircraft surfaces for protection against elements and for aesthetic purposes. Reinforced laminates, on the other hand, are laminates that have additional reinforcement materials such as fibers or fabric embedded within the layers for added strength and durability. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the performance, appearance, and longevity of aircraft components.</p></p>
