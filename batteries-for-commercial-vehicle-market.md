<p><p>The flooded lead-acid batteries market is characterized by traditional batteries that have been widely used in commercial vehicles for many years. These batteries contain a liquid electrolyte that immerses the lead plates, providing reliable power but requiring regular maintenance. On the other hand, absorbent glass mat (AGM) batteries offer a more advanced solution with a mat-like separator that absorbs the electrolyte, allowing for maintenance-free operation and improved performance. AGM batteries are gaining popularity in the commercial vehicle market due to their durability and efficiency.</p></p>
