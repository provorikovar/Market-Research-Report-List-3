<p><p>Ternary precursor materials are categorized into two main types - NCM type and NCA type. NCM type materials are made up of nickel, cobalt, and manganese, while NCA type materials consist of nickel, cobalt, and aluminum. NCM type materials are more commonly used in lithium-ion batteries for electric vehicles due to their higher energy density and stability, while NCA type materials are known for their high specific capacity and thermal stability. Both types play a crucial role in the development of advanced battery technologies.</p></p>
