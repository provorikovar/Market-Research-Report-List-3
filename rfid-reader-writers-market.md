<p><p>RFID Reader-Writers Market consists of different types based on the frequency they operate on. LF RFID Reader-Writers mainly operate on low frequencies, HF RFID Reader-Writers operate on high frequencies, UHF RFID Reader-Writers operate on ultra-high frequencies, and MW RFID Reader-Writers operate on microwaves. Each type caters to specific applications and industries, with differences in reading range, data transfer speed, and compatibility. LF is commonly used for access control, HF for payment systems, UHF for supply chain management, and MW for identification in harsh environments.</p></p>
