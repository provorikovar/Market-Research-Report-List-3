<p><p>Image detection sensor market consists of various types such as CCD, CMOS, and thermal sensors. CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) sensors offer high image quality and low noise levels, making them ideal for applications requiring precise image detection. CMOS sensors are more cost-effective and energy-efficient, making them popular in consumer electronics. Thermal sensors are used for detecting heat signatures and are commonly used in night vision cameras and security systems. Each type has its own advantages and applications within the image detection sensor market.</p></p>
