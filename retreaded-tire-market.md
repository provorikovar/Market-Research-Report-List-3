<p><p>The retreaded tire market consists of three main types: radial tires, bias tires, and solid tires. Radial tires are designed with ply cords that run at a 90-degree angle to the direction of travel, offering greater flexibility and improved traction. Bias tires have ply cords that run at an angle to the direction of travel, making them more rigid and durable. Solid tires are made of solid rubber, providing excellent resistance to punctures and wear. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
