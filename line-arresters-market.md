<p><p>Line Arresters Market can be categorized into three main types based on the voltage range they are designed to protect: below 35 KV, between 35 KV and 110 KV, and above 110 KV. Each type of arrester is specifically engineered to effectively protect power lines within their designated voltage range from overvoltage events. Line arresters below 35 KV are commonly used in residential and small commercial applications, while those between 35 KV and 110 KV are utilized in medium voltage distribution systems. Line arresters above 110 KV are typically used in high voltage transmission lines.</p></p>
