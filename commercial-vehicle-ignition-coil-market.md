<p><strong>The Commercial Vehicle Ignition Coil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-spark</li><li>Multi-spark</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial vehicle ignition coils are available in two main types: single-spark and multi-spark. Single-spark ignition coils produce a single spark to ignite the air-fuel mixture in the engine cylinder, while multi-spark coils generate multiple sparks in quick succession. Single-spark coils are simpler and more cost-effective, while multi-spark coils provide better fuel efficiency and improved engine performance. Both types of ignition coils have their own advantages and are used in different commercial vehicles based on specific requirements.</p></p>
