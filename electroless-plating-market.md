<p><p>Electroless plating, also known as autocatalytic plating, is a process of depositing a thin layer of metal onto a metal substrate without the use of an external electrical current. This process involves chemical reaction instead of electricity to coat the surface of the substrate, providing even and uniform coverage. Electroless plating is widely used in various industries such as automotive, electronics, aerospace, and manufacturing for applications like corrosion resistance, solderability, and wear resistance.</p><p>The Electroless Plating Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11% during the forecast period.
