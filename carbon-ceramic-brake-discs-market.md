<p><ul><li>Drilled Carbon-ceramic Brake Discs</li><li>Slotted Carbon-ceramic Brake Discs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Drilled carbon-ceramic brake discs have holes drilled into the surface, which help dissipate heat more efficiently and reduce brake fade. This type of disc is popular among performance enthusiasts for its high-performance capabilities. On the other hand, slotted carbon-ceramic brake discs have slots machined into the surface, which improve brake bite and allow for better water evacuation, making them ideal for wet conditions. Both types offer superior braking performance and durability compared to traditional brake discs.</p></p>
