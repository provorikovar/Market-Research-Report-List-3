Chlorine gas is utilized in dry etching processes, and silicon gases are essential for chemical vapor deposition. Ammonia gas is commonly used for cleaning and doping processes. Other specialty gases like fluorine, argon, and helium are also used in semiconductor manufacturing for specific processes such as ion implantation and thermal processing.</p></p>
