<p><p>Positive Temperature Coefficient Resistors (PTCR) are resistors that increase their resistance with an increase in temperature. There are two main types of PTCR in the market: Polymer PTC and Ceramic PTC. Polymer PTC resistors are made of a conductive polymer material and are commonly used in electronics for overcurrent protection. Ceramic PTC resistors are made of a ceramic material and are known for their high stability and reliability. Both types of PTCR are widely used in various industries for temperature sensing and control applications.</p></p>
