<p><p>Rotational moulding powders market can be classified into three main types: Polyethylene, Polycarbonate, and PVC Plastisol. Polyethylene powders are the most commonly used due to their flexibility, durability, and cost-effectiveness. Polycarbonate powders offer high impact strength and transparency, making them suitable for products requiring these properties. PVC Plastisol powders are ideal for applications requiring high chemical resistance and good thermal stability. Each type of powder caters to different product requirements and industries, providing diverse options for rotational moulding processes.</p></p>
