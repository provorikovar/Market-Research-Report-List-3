<p><strong>The Imaging Colorimeters & Photometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD-based</li><li>CMOS-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Imaging colorimeters and photometers are devices used to measure color and light intensity. There are two main types of imaging sensors used in these devices: CCD-based and CMOS-based. CCD-based sensors traditionally offer higher sensitivity and better signal-to-noise ratio, making them more suitable for applications requiring high precision. On the other hand, CMOS-based sensors are known for their lower cost and faster readout speeds, making them ideal for high-speed imaging applications. The choice between these two types of sensors depends on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
