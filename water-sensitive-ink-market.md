<p><p>Water sensitive ink has two main market types: Humidity Sensitive Apochromatic Ink (Reversible) and Humidity Sensitive Color Spreading Ink. Humidity Sensitive Apochromatic Ink changes color in response to humidity levels, but can revert back to its original color when conditions change. On the other hand, Humidity Sensitive Color Spreading Ink spreads and changes color when exposed to moisture, creating unique and dynamic designs. Both types offer innovative and interactive features that make them popular choices for various applications.</p></p>
