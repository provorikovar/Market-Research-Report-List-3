<p><ul><li>Salt Lake Source</li><li>Mine Source</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The boron market can be classified into two main types: Salt Lake Source and Mine Source. Salt Lake Source boron is extracted from salt brines in evaporative basins, typically found in countries like Turkey and the United States. This type of boron is known for its purity and is often used in high-tech applications. On the other hand, Mine Source boron is extracted from boron minerals in underground mines, with major producers located in countries like Turkey and Russia. Mine Source boron is known for its high grade and abundance.</p></p>
