<p><p>The spectroscopy equipment market can be classified into three main types: molecular spectroscopy equipment, atomic spectroscopy equipment, and mass spectroscopy equipment. Molecular spectroscopy equipment is used to study the interaction of molecules with electromagnetic radiation. Atomic spectroscopy equipment analyzes the elemental composition of samples by measuring the absorption and emission of light by atoms. Mass spectroscopy equipment identifies and quantifies the chemical compounds present in a sample by measuring the mass-to-charge ratios of ions. Each type of spectroscopy equipment offers unique capabilities for analyzing different types of samples.</p></p>
