<p><ul><li>Laser Produced Plasmas</li><li>Vacuum Sparks</li><li>Gas Discharges</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Extreme Ultraviolet Lithography (EUVL) Systems market consists of three main types of sources: Laser Produced Plasmas, Vacuum Sparks, and Gas Discharges. Laser Produced Plasmas use high-energy laser beams to create plasma that emits EUV light. Vacuum Sparks use an electric discharge in a vacuum chamber to generate EUV radiation. Gas Discharges involve using a gas-filled chamber with an electric discharge to produce EUV light. These technologies are utilized in the semiconductor industry for advanced lithography processes.</p></p>
