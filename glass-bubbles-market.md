<p><p>Glass bubbles market can be classified into two main types: Hollow Glass Bubbles and Solid Glass Bubbles. Hollow Glass Bubbles are lightweight, low-density microspheres with thin walls that provide excellent insulation properties and are commonly used in applications such as aerospace, construction, and automotive industries. On the other hand, Solid Glass Bubbles are solid microspheres that offer high compressive strength and are often used in applications where thermal conductivity and mechanical strength are crucial, such as in the oil and gas industry.</p></p>
