<p><p>Glass frit powder is available in two main types: coarse powder and fine powder. Coarse powder is typically larger in grain size and is commonly used for creating textured surfaces or providing a rougher finish. Fine powder, on the other hand, has a smaller grain size and is often used for applications requiring a smooth and polished surface. Both types of glass frit powder offer unique benefits and are chosen based on the desired finish and application requirements.</p></p>
