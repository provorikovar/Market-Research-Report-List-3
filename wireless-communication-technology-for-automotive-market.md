<p><p>Wireless Communication Technology for Automotive Market includes DSRC (Dedicated Short Range Communication) and Mesh networks. DSRC allows vehicles to communicate with each other and with roadside infrastructure for improving safety and efficiency. Mesh networks enable vehicles to form a network where data can hop from one vehicle to another, enhancing communication reliability and expanding coverage. Both technologies play a crucial role in enabling advanced car-to-car and car-to-infrastructure communication, paving the way for the future of connected and autonomous vehicles.</p></p>
