<p><ul><li>Optical Imaging</li><li>Radionuclide Imaging</li><li>MRI</li><li>Computed Tomography Imaging</li><li>Ultrasound Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Small animal imaging equipment market encompasses various types of imaging modalities used for preclinical research on small animals. Optical imaging utilizes light to visualize anatomical and functional features. Radionuclide imaging involves the use of radioactive tracers for molecular imaging. MRI provides detailed structural and functional information using magnetic fields. Computed tomography imaging produces cross-sectional images using X-rays. Ultrasound imaging uses sound waves to create real-time images. Each modality offers unique advantages for studying disease progression, drug efficacy, and biological processes in small animals.</p></p>
