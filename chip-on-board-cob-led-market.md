<p><strong>The Chip-on-board  (CoB) LED Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lateral Chip</li><li>Vertical Chip</li><li>Flip Chip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chip-on-board (CoB) LED market types include lateral chip, vertical chip, and flip chip designs. Lateral chip CoB LEDs have the LED chip positioned horizontally on the substrate, providing a wide light distribution. Vertical chip CoB LEDs have the LED chip vertically mounted on the substrate for more focused lighting. Flip chip CoB LEDs have the LED chip flipped upside down and mounted directly onto the substrate, reducing heat generation and improving thermal management. Each market type offers unique characteristics suited for different applications and lighting requirements.</p></p>
