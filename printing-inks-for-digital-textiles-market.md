<p><p>Printing inks for digital textiles are available in various types to cater to different printing requirements. Reactive dye inks are ideal for printing on natural fibers like cotton, providing vibrant colors and excellent washfastness. Acidic inks are suitable for printing on silk and wool, offering vivid hues and good lightfastness. Paint inks are used for direct printing on fabrics, creating a hand-painted effect. Dispersion inks are water-based and environmentally friendly, while sublimation inks are used for printing on synthetic fabrics through a heat transfer process, ensuring long-lasting designs.</p></p>
