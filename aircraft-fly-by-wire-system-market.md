<p><strong>The Aircraft Fly-by-wire System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Flight Control Backup Systems</li><li>Fully Fly-By-Wire Controls Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>An Aircraft Fly-by-wire System consists of two main types of systems. The first is Mechanical Flight Control Backup Systems, which utilize traditional mechanical controls as a backup in case the fly-by-wire system fails. The second type is Fully Fly-By-Wire Controls Systems, which completely rely on electronic controls for flight operations. While Mechanical Flight Control Backup Systems provide redundancy and reliability, Fully Fly-By-Wire Controls Systems offer advanced capabilities and increased efficiency in aircraft operations. Both systems play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and performance of modern aircraft.</p></p>
