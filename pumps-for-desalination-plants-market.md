<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Pumps</li><li>Positive Displacement Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Centrifugal pumps are commonly used in desalination plants for their high flow rates and efficiency in moving large volumes of water. They are ideal for applications where a continuous and steady flow of water is required. On the other hand, positive displacement pumps are preferred for their ability to maintain a constant flow rate, especially in situations where water pressure needs to be consistent. Both types of pumps play a crucial role in desalination plants by helping to optimize the process of converting saltwater into freshwater.</p></p>
