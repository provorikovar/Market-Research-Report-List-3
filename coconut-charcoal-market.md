<p><p>Coconut charcoal is available in two main market types: lump and powder. Lump charcoal consists of large chunks of charcoal that burn for a longer period of time and produce a steady heat. It is commonly used in grilling and smoking meats. On the other hand, coconut charcoal powder is finely ground charcoal that is used for various purposes such as teeth whitening, beauty products, and as an odor absorber. Both types of coconut charcoal offer different benefits and applications for consumers.</p></p>
