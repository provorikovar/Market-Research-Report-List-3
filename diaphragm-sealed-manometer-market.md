<p><p>Diaphragm Sealed Manometer market includes various types such as U Tube Manometers and Inclined Tube Manometers. U Tube Manometers have a U-shaped tube with one end connected to the pressure source and the other end open to the atmosphere, allowing for accurate measurement of pressure differentials. Inclined Tube Manometers have a tilted tube that allows for easier reading of the pressure difference. Both types are commonly used in various industries for measuring and monitoring pressure levels accurately and efficiently.</p></p>
