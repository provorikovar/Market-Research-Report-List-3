<p><p>Welding consumables for nuclear power market includes shielded metal arc welding, submerged arc welding, and other types. Shielded metal arc welding utilizes a consumable electrode coated in flux to protect the weld pool from contaminants. Submerged arc welding involves feeding a continuous wire electrode under a layer of granular flux to shield the weld. Other types may include gas metal arc welding, gas tungsten arc welding, and flux-cored arc welding, each offering unique advantages for welding in nuclear power applications.</p></p>
