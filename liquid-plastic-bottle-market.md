<p><p>The liquid plastic bottle market can be categorized into different types based on the material used for manufacturing. The common types include PET (polyethylene terephthalate), PP (polypropylene), HDPE (high-density polyethylene), LDPE (low-density polyethylene), and others. PET bottles are commonly used for beverages and personal care products, while PP bottles are suitable for packaging pharmaceuticals. HDPE bottles are known for their durability and are often used for industrial and household products. LDPE bottles are more flexible and are often used for squeeze bottles and dispensing containers.</p></p>
