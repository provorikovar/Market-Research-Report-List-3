<p><p>Wafer Level Packaging Inspection Systems utilize advanced technology to inspect the quality of wafers at a microscopic level. Two common types of inspection systems are Optical Based and Infrared Type. Optical Based systems use visible light to capture images of the wafer surface and analyze defects, while Infrared Type systems detect defects based on differences in thermal emissions. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and reliability of semiconductor devices produced through wafer level packaging processes.</p></p>
