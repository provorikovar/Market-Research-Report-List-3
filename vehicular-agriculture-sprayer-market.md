<p><p>Vehicular agriculture sprayers can be categorized into two main types: engine drive sprayers and motor drive sprayers. Engine drive sprayers are powered by an internal combustion engine, providing high power for large-scale agricultural applications. On the other hand, motor drive sprayers use an electric motor for operation, offering more cost-effective and environmentally friendly solutions for smaller farming operations. Both types of sprayers play a crucial role in efficiently applying fertilizers and pesticides to crops, contributing to increased agricultural productivity.</p></p>
