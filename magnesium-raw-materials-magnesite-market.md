<p><p>Phanerocrystalline magnesite refers to a type of magnesium raw material that has visible crystals within its structure. This type of magnesite is often used for industrial purposes due to its high purity levels. On the other hand, cryptocrystalline magnesite is a type of magnesite that has microscopic crystals or lacks visible crystal structure. This type of magnesite is commonly used for refractory applications and has slightly lower purity levels compared to phanerocrystalline magnesite.</p></p>
