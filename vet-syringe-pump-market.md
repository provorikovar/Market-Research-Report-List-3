<p><p>Vet syringe pumps come in two main market types: 1-Channel and 2-Channel. A 1-Channel syringe pump allows for the administration of fluids or medication through a single syringe at a time. The choice between the two types depends on the specific needs of the veterinarian and the medical treatment being provided to the animal.</p></p>
