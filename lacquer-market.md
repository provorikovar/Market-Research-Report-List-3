<p><strong>The Lacquer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-based Lacquer</li><li>Water-based Lacquer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lacquer market is divided into two main types: solvent-based lacquer and water-based lacquer. Solvent-based lacquers are made with chemicals that evaporate during the drying process, creating a hard and durable finish. They are known for their fast drying time and high resistance to heat and chemicals. On the other hand, water-based lacquers are formulated with water as the main solvent, making them more environmentally friendly and low in VOCs. They offer a smoother finish and are easier to clean up. Both types have their own advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
