<p><p>Polyethersulfone (PES) is a high-performance thermoplastic known for its excellent thermal and mechanical properties. The market for PES includes two main types: PES Powder Grades and PES Granule Grades. PES Powder Grades are fine powders used in various end-use applications such as coatings, adhesives, and composites. On the other hand, PES Granule Grades are granular materials typically used in injection molding and extrusion processes to produce components for industries like aerospace, automotive, and electronics. Both types of PES offer high heat resistance, chemical resistance, and dimensional stability.</p></p>
