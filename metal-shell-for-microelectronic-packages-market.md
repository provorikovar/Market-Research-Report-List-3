<p><strong>The Metal Shell for Microelectronic Packages Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TO Shell</li><li>Flat Shell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal shell packaging for microelectronics comes in two main types - TO shell and flat shell. TO shell packages have a cylindrical metal shell with a flat bottom and a  lead frame for connecting external circuits. They are commonly used for power devices. Flat shell packages, on the other hand, have a flat metal shell with leads coming out from the sides or corners. They are often used for integrated circuits and other small electronic components. Both types provide protection and electrical connection for the delicate microelectronics inside.</p></p>
