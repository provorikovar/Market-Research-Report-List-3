<p><strong>The Automobile Airbag Inflators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pyrotechnic</li><li>Stored Gas</li><li>Hybrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automobile airbag inflators come in three main types: pyrotechnic, stored gas, and hybrid. Pyrotechnic inflators use a chemical reaction to rapidly generate gas to inflate the airbag. Stored gas inflators store compressed gas that is released upon impact to inflate the airbag. Hybrid inflators combine elements of both pyrotechnic and stored gas systems for efficient and reliable inflation. Each type of inflator has its own advantages and characteristics, catering to different preferences and requirements in the automotive industry.</p></p>
