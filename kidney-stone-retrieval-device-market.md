Lithotripters are devices used to break up kidney stones using shock waves. Stone Removal Devices are instruments designed to physically remove kidney stones from the urinary tract. Ureteral stents are flexible tubes inserted into the ureter to help urine drain from the kidney to the bladder. Each type of device plays a crucial role in the treatment and management of kidney stones.</p></p>
