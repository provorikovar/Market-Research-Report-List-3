<p><p>Electrosurgical electrodes come in various types including blade type, loop type, needle/ultra-fine, and ball type. Blade type electrodes are used for cutting tissue, while loop type electrodes are used for coagulating or removing tissue. Needle or ultra-fine electrodes are designed for delicate procedures, and ball type electrodes are used for precise coagulation. Each type of electrode offers unique benefits and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the surgical procedure.</p></p>
