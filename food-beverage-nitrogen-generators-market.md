<p><strong>The Food & Beverage Nitrogen Generators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PSA Nitrogen Generators</li><li>Membrane Nitrogen Generators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PSA Nitrogen Generators use Pressure Swing Adsorption technology to separate nitrogen gas from other gases in the air, making it suitable for food and beverage applications. Membrane Nitrogen Generators, on the other hand, use selective permeation through membrane filters to produce high-purity nitrogen gas. Both types of nitrogen generators are commonly used in the food and beverage industry to extend the shelf life of products, prevent oxidation, and improve product quality by displacing oxygen in packaging and storage environments.</p></p>
