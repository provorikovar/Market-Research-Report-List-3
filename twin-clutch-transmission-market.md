<p><strong>The Twin-clutch Transmission Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Multiplate Clutches</li><li>Dry Single-Plate Clutches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Twin-clutch transmissions are available in two types: wet multiplate clutches and dry single-plate clutches. Wet multiplate clutches use oil for cooling and lubrication, making them ideal for high-performance vehicles. On the other hand, dry single-plate clutches do not use oil, making them lighter and more compact, suitable for everyday driving. Both types offer fast and seamless gear shifting by pre-selecting the next gear to improve efficiency and performance in automotive applications.</p></p>
