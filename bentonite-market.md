<p><p>Bentonite market is divided into two main types - Sodium Bentonite and Calcium Bentonite. Sodium Bentonite is known for its high swelling capacity when mixed with water, making it suitable for sealing and waterproofing applications. On the other hand, Calcium Bentonite has a lower swelling capacity but is preferred for its detoxifying properties in skincare products and dietary supplements. Both types of Bentonite are widely used in various industries such as construction, agriculture, and cosmetics due to their unique properties and benefits.</p></p>
