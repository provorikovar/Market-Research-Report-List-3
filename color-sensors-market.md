<p><strong>The Color Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monocolour Sensors</li><li>RGB Color Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Color sensors can be classified into two main types: Monocolour sensors and RGB color sensors. Monocolour sensors are capable of detecting and measuring only one specific color, making them suitable for applications that require the identification of a single color. On the other hand, RGB color sensors are able to detect and differentiate between three primary colors (Red, Green, Blue), allowing for more accurate color detection and measurement. Both types of color sensors play a crucial role in various industries such as automotive, consumer electronics, and printing.</p></p>
