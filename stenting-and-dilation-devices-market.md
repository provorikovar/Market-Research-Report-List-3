<p><p>Stenting and dilation devices are medical devices used to treat narrowed or blocked blood vessels, airways, or other tubular structures in the body. These devices are commonly used in the treatment of conditions such as coronary artery disease, peripheral artery disease, and gastrointestinal obstructions.</p><p>The global market for stenting and dilation devices is expected to grow significantly in the coming years, with a projected compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 13.7% during the forecast period.
