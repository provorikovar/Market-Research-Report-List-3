<p><p>Mounted agriculture sprayers come in two main types: Engine Drive Sprayers and Motor Drive Sprayers. Engine Drive Sprayers are equipped with an engine that directly powers the spraying system, providing a reliable and efficient method for applying pesticides and fertilizers. On the other hand, Motor Drive Sprayers use a motor to drive the spraying system, offering a more compact and versatile option for agricultural operations. Both types of sprayers play a crucial role in modern farming practices by ensuring effective and precise application of inputs.</p></p>
