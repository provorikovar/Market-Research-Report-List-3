<p><p>ESD diodes market includes various types such as Zener Diodes, Varistors, Silicon Controlled Rectifiers, Transient Voltage Suppressors, and others. Zener diodes provide voltage regulation, Varistors protect against overvoltage surges, Silicon Controlled Rectifiers are used for switching and rectifying applications, Transient Voltage Suppressors protect against voltage spikes. Other types may include TVS diodes, Schottky diodes, or ESD protection diodes. Each type of ESD diode serves a specific purpose in protecting electronic circuits from electrostatic discharge events.</p></p>
