<p><p>Silicone liquid rubbers are available in two main market types: one-component and two-component. One-component rubbers are pre-mixed and ready to use, offering convenience and simplicity in applications. On the other hand, two-component rubbers require mixing of two separate components before use, offering more control over the curing process and allowing for customization of properties. Both market types cater to different needs and preferences of users, with one-component rubbers being suitable for quick and easy applications while two-component rubbers offer more versatility and precision.</p></p>
