<p><strong>The BOPET Packaging Films Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thickness: Below 15 μm</li><li>Thickness: 15-30 μm</li><li>Thickness: 30-50 μm</li><li>Thickness: Above 50 μm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>BOPET packaging films are categorized based on their thickness into four main types: below 15 μm, 15-30 μm, 30-50 μm, and above 50 μm. Films below 15 μm are often used for applications requiring lightweight and flexible packaging. Films in the 15-30 μm range offer a balance between flexibility and strength, making them suitable for various packaging needs. Films in the 30-50 μm range are ideal for heavier or more durable packaging requirements. Films above 50 μm are the thickest and provide the highest level of strength and protection for packaging products.</p></p>
