<p><p>Power sports vehicles can be categorized into four main types: ATV (all-terrain vehicle), UTV (utility task vehicle), motorcycle, and snowmobile. ATVs are designed for off-road use, with four wheels and a handlebar for steering. UTVs are larger, with a steering wheel and seating for multiple passengers, making them ideal for utility work or recreational riding. Motorcycles are two-wheeled vehicles used for commuting, touring, or racing. Snowmobiles are designed for winter travel over snow and ice, typically with skis at the front and a track at the rear.</p></p>
