<p><ul><li>Flat Cables</li><li>Circular-liked Cables</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Elevator travel cables come in two main types: flat cables and circular-linked cables. Flat cables are made up of multiple conductors assembled side by side in a flat configuration, providing flexibility and durability. Circular-linked cables, on the other hand, consist of multiple conductors twisted together in a circular pattern, offering higher flexibility and resistance to bending. Both types of cables are essential components in elevator systems, enabling smooth and safe vertical travel.</p></p>
