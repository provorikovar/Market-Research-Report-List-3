<p><p>CMP Polishing Liquid market consists of different types of slurries, including Alumina Slurry, Colloidal Silica Slurry, and Ceria Slurries. Alumina Slurry is commonly used for oxide layers, while Colloidal Silica Slurry is preferred for metal layers due to its abrasiveness. Ceria Slurries are known for their high removal rate and ability to achieve a smooth surface finish. Each type of slurry caters to specific material removal needs in the semiconductor industry, ensuring efficient and precise CMP processes.</p></p>
