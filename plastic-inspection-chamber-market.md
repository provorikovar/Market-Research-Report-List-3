<p><p>Plastic Inspection Chambers are divided into two main types in the market: Rotational Molding Plastic Inspection Chambers and Welded Piping Plastic Inspection Chambers. Rotational Molding Chambers are manufactured using a rotational molding process, which results in a seamless and durable product that is resistant to corrosion and other environmental factors. On the other hand, Welded Piping Chambers are constructed using welded piping, offering a more customizable and flexible solution for various underground utility applications. Both types of chambers provide reliable and efficient solutions for inspecting and accessing underground utility networks.</p></p>
