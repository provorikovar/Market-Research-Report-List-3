<p><strong>The Water Leak Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Type</li><li>Non-contacting Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Water leak sensors are classified into contact type and non-contacting type. Contact type sensors come in direct contact with the water to detect leaks, while non-contacting type sensors use various technologies such as ultrasound or infrared to detect water without direct contact. Contact type sensors are more commonly used in residential settings for their simplicity and affordability, while non-contacting type sensors are often favored for their high accuracy and ability to cover larger areas in industrial or commercial applications.</p></p>
