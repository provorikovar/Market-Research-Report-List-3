<p><p>Polydimethylsiloxane (PDMS) is commonly used in cosmetics due to its various viscosity levels. Median Viscosity PDMS strikes a balance between texture and spreadability, often found in serums and primers. High Viscosity PDMS is thicker and more substantial, commonly used in products like foundations and concealers for long-lasting coverage. Each viscosity level caters to different cosmetic needs, offering a range of options for formulation and application.</p></p>
