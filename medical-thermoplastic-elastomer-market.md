<p><strong>The Medical Thermoplastic Elastomer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic Polyurethanes (TPU)</li><li>Styrene Block Copolyme (SBC)</li><li>Thermoplastic Vulcanizates (TPV)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical thermoplastic elastomers are widely categorized into three types: Thermoplastic Polyurethanes (TPU), Styrene Block Copolymers (SBC), and Thermoplastic Vulcanizates (TPV). TPUs are known for their high flexibility, durability, and resistance to abrasion and chemicals. SBCs offer excellent elasticity and are often used in medical devices that require softness and flexibility. TPVs combine the properties of rubber with the processing ease of thermoplastics, making them ideal for applications that require a balance of flexibility and toughness.</p></p>
