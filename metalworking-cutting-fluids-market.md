<p><strong>The Metalworking Cutting Fluids Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Neat Oil Metal Cutting Fluids</li><li>Emulsion Metal Cutting Fluids</li><li>Semi-Synthetic Metal Cutting Fluids</li><li>Synthesis Metal Cutting Fluids</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metalworking cutting fluids are used to reduce friction, heat, and tool wear during machining processes. Neat oil metal cutting fluids are undiluted oils, suitable for heavy-duty cutting operations. Emulsion metal cutting fluids are water-based mixtures of oil and emulsifiers, offering good cooling properties. Semi-synthetic metal cutting fluids are a mixture of oil and water, providing a balance between lubrication and cooling. Synthetic metal cutting fluids are chemically engineered fluids, offering high performance and environmental benefits. Each type of cutting fluid caters to different machining needs and preferences.</p></p>
