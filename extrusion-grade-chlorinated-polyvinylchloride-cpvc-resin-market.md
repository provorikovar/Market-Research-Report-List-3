<p><p>Extrusion grade Chlorinated Polyvinylchloride (CPVC) resin is available in two main types based on the chlorine content: high chlorine and low chloride. High chlorine CPVC resin has a higher chlorine content, typically above 65%, which results in improved heat resistance, chemical resistance, and flame retardancy. On the other hand, low chloride CPVC resin has a lower chlorine content, below 65%, making it more suitable for applications where corrosion resistance is a priority. Both types offer excellent processability and durability for extrusion applications.</p></p>
