<p><p>Milk Kefir is a fermented dairy beverage with a tart and tangy taste, similar to yogurt but thinner in consistency. Milk Kefir is known for its potential to improve digestion, boost immunity, and support overall gut health.</p><p>The Milk Kefir Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.9% during the forecast period, driven by the increasing consumer awareness about the health benefits of probiotic-rich foods and beverages.
