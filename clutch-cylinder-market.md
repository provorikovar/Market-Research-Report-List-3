<p><ul><li>Clutch Master Cylinder(CMC)</li><li>Clutch Release Cylinder(CRC)</li><li>Clutch Slave Cylinder (CSC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Clutch Cylinder market consists of three main types: Clutch Master Cylinder (CMC), Clutch Release Cylinder (CRC), and Clutch Slave Cylinder (CSC). The CMC is responsible for transferring the force from the clutch pedal to the clutch release system. The CRC is used to disengage the clutch when the pedal is pressed. The CSC is responsible for disengaging the clutch when the hydraulic pressure is applied. Each type plays a crucial role in the proper functioning of a vehicle's clutch system.</p></p>
