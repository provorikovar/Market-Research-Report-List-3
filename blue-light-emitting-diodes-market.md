<p><p>Blue Light-emitting Diodes are commonly used in two main types of displays: PMOLED (Passive Matrix Organic Light Emitting Diode) and AMOLED (Active Matrix Organic Light Emitting Diode) displays. PMOLED displays are simpler and cheaper to produce, making them ideal for small-sized screens like smartwatches and wearable devices. On the other hand, AMOLED displays offer higher resolution and better energy efficiency, making them popular for larger screens like smartphones and televisions. Both types of displays use blue LEDs to produce vibrant and energy-efficient blue light.</p></p>
