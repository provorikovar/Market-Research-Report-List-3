<p><p>Chlorine Dioxide Generator Market can be categorized into two main types: Electrolytic Method and Chemical Method. In the Electrolytic Method, chlorine dioxide is generated by passing an electric current through a solution containing sodium chlorite. This method is commonly used in industries such as water treatment. On the other hand, the Chemical Method involves the reaction of sodium chlorite with a specific acid to produce chlorine dioxide. This method is often preferred for smaller-scale applications such as disinfection in healthcare facilities.</p></p>
