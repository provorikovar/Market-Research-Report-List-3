<p><p>CNC machine tools market can be divided into three main types: CNC Lathe, CNC Milling machine, and CNC Grinding machine. CNC Lathe is used for shaping materials by rotating the workpiece against cutting tools. CNC Milling machine is used for cutting and drilling solid materials by moving the workpiece along multiple axes. CNC Grinding machine is used for grinding and finishing materials with a rotating grinding wheel. These machines are widely used in industries like automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing for precision and efficiency in production processes.</p></p>
