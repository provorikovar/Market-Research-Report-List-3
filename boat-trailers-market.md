<p><ul><li>Bunk</li><li>Roller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Boat trailers come in two main types - bunk trailers and roller trailers. Bunk trailers use carpeted or rubber pads to support the boat's hull, while roller trailers have multiple rollers that support the boat's weight. Bunk trailers are better for boats with V-shaped hulls, providing more surface area for support, while roller trailers are more convenient for easy launching and loading, especially in shallow waters. Both types have their advantages and are popular choices in the boat trailer market.</p></p>
