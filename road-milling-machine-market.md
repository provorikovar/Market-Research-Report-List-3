<p><ul><li>Wheeled Road Milling Machine</li><li>Tracked Road Milling Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Road milling machines are categorized into two types: wheeled road milling machines and tracked road milling machines. Wheeled road milling machines have wheels attached to their base, making them mobile and easy to maneuver on flat surfaces. Tracked road milling machines, on the other hand, are equipped with tracks instead of wheels, allowing them to navigate rough and uneven terrains with ease. Both types of machines are used for removing layers of asphalt or concrete from roads to make them smooth and even for driving.</p></p>
