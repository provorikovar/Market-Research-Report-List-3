<p><p>Graphite Fiber Felt is classified into three main types based on the precursor material used during its manufacturing process: PAN (polyacrylonitrile) Graphite Felt, Rayon Graphite Felt, and Pitch Graphite Felt. PAN Graphite Felt is derived from polyacrylonitrile polymer, Rayon Graphite Felt is produced from regenerated cellulose fibers, and Pitch Graphite Felt is made from coal tar pitch. Each type of Graphite Fiber Felt has unique properties and applications, making them suitable for a variety of industries such as aerospace, automotive, and energy.</p></p>
