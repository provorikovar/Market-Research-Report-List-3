<p><p>Liquid chromatography-mass spectroscopy market types include triple quadrupole, time of flight, quadrupole-time of flight, and others. Triple quadrupole mass spectrometry allows for high sensitivity and selectivity in analyzing compounds. Time of flight mass spectrometry measures the time it takes for ions to travel a known distance, providing accurate mass determination. Quadrupole-time of flight combines the selectivity of quadrupole with the accuracy of time of flight. Other types of mass spectrometers may include ion trap or magnetic sector instruments.</p></p>
