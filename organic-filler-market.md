<p><p>The organic filler market can be divided into two main types - thermoset and thermoplastic. Thermoset organic fillers are materials that undergo a chemical reaction during processing, leading to a permanent change in their structure. They are often used in applications requiring high heat and chemical resistance. On the other hand, thermoplastic organic fillers can be melted and reformed multiple times, making them more versatile in a wider range of industries. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the properties of various materials.</p></p>
