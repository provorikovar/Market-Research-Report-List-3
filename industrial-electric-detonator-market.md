<p><p>Industrial Electric Detonator market is segmented into two main types: Instant Electric Detonator and Delay Electric Detonator. Instant Electric Detonators are designed to provide immediate ignition upon activation, commonly used in applications where precise timing is not necessary. Delay Electric Detonators, on the other hand, are equipped with a delay mechanism that allows for a specific time lapse before detonation, making them ideal for applications requiring precise timing such as mining and construction. Both types play a crucial role in the blasting industry.</p></p>
