<p><ul><li>Manual</li><li>Semi-automatic</li><li>Fully Automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Food tray sealers are available in three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual tray sealers require operators to manually place trays onto the machine and operate the sealing process. Semi-automatic tray sealers automate the sealing process but still require some manual handling. Fully automatic tray sealers are the most efficient and hands-free option, with trays being automatically loaded, sealed, and unloaded without the need for manual intervention. Each type of tray sealer caters to different production scales and requirements.</p></p>
