<p><p>IO-Link Communication Systems consist of IO-Link Master, IO-Link Sensor, and Other markets. IO-Link Master acts as a gateway between sensors and the control system, facilitating bi-directional communication. IO-Link Sensor refers to the sensors that communicate with the IO-Link Master. The Other market includes various devices compatible with IO-Link technology, such as actuators and RFID systems. Together, these components form a comprehensive communication system that enables seamless information exchange between devices in industrial automation applications.</p></p>
