<p><p>Edible whipping cream market offers various types catering to different consumer preferences. Light whipping cream contains around 30-36% fat content, making it ideal for topping desserts without being too heavy. Heavy whipping cream has a fat content of 36-40%, providing a rich and thick texture for whipping. Double cream contains at least 48% fat, resulting in a luxurious and velvety consistency. Manufacturing cream is a higher fat version of light whipping cream, with a fat content of at least 39%.</p></p>
