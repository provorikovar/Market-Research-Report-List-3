<p><p>The Mobile Capacitor Bank market can be categorized into two main types: Single Step and Multi Step. Single Step Capacitor Banks are designed with a single capacitor unit and are suitable for smaller applications where quick adjustments in power factor correction are needed. On the other hand, Multi Step Capacitor Banks consist of multiple capacitor units that can be switched on or off in sequence, allowing for more precise power factor correction in larger and more complex electrical systems. Each type offers unique advantages depending on the specific needs of the application.</p></p>
