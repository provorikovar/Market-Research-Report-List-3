<p><p>Belt driven conveyors are classified into two main types: light belt driven and heavy belt driven. Light belt driven conveyors are designed for handling smaller loads and are commonly used in industries such as food processing and packaging. They are typically equipped with smaller, lightweight belts. Heavy belt driven conveyors, on the other hand, are designed for handling larger and heavier loads, such as bulk materials and automotive parts. They are equipped with larger, durable belts to withstand the weight and impact of heavy items.</p></p>
