<p><p>Video recorders market can be categorized into three main types: Embedded DVRs, Hybrid DVRs, and PC-based DVRs. Embedded DVRs are standalone systems with built-in hardware and software, made for easy installation and use. Hybrid DVRs combine analog and IP camera capabilities, providing flexibility for users. PC-based DVRs utilize computer systems for recording and storage, offering high levels of customization and scalability. Each market type caters to different needs and preferences of users in terms of functionality and performance.</p></p>
