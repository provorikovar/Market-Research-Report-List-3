<p><p>Industrial Gas Alarm Market consists of various types, including Catalytic Type and Infrared Optical. Catalytic Type alarms detect gases through a catalytic combustion sensor, which reacts with gases and triggers an alarm. On the other hand, Infrared Optical alarms use an infrared light source to detect gases based on their absorption of infrared radiation. Both types are commonly used in industrial settings to ensure the safety of workers and prevent potential gas leaks or hazards.</p></p>
