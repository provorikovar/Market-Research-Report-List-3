<p><strong>The Probe Card Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cantilever Probe Card</li><li>Vertical Probe Card</li><li>MEMS Probe Card</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Probe cards are essential tools in the semiconductor industry for testing Integrated Circuits (ICs) during the manufacturing process. The different types of probe cards include Cantilever Probe Cards, Vertical Probe Cards, and MEMS Probe Cards. Cantilever Probe Cards use flexible beams to make contact with the ICs, Vertical Probe Cards have pins that move up and down to make contact, and MEMS Probe Cards use Micro-Electro-Mechanical-Systems to achieve high precision testing. Each type of probe card offers unique advantages and is suited for specific testing requirements in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
