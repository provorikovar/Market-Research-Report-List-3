<p><p>Anaerobic thread sealants are categorized into three types based on their strength levels - low, medium, and high strength. Low strength sealants are designed for easy disassembly of parts without damaging threads. Medium strength sealants offer a balance between ease of disassembly and secure sealing. High strength sealants are used for permanent bonds and are ideal for applications where parts need to be securely sealed and prevented from loosening. Each type of sealant caters to different levels of strength requirements depending on the application.</p></p>
