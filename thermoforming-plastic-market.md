<p><strong>The Thermoforming Plastic Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vacuum Formed</li><li>Pressure Formed</li><li>Mechanical Formed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermoforming plastic market consists of three main types: Vacuum Formed, Pressure Formed, and Mechanical Formed. Vacuum forming involves heating a plastic sheet until it is pliable, then using a vacuum to shape it over a mold. Pressure forming uses both heat and pressure to mold the plastic sheet over a mold. Mechanical forming, on the other hand, relies on mechanical force to shape the plastic sheet. Each type of thermoforming offers unique advantages and is used in various industries for producing a wide range of products.</p></p>
