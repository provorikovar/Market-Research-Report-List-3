<p><p>The Electronic Doorbell market can be categorized into three main types - Wired Doorbell, Wireless Visible Doorbell, and Wireless Invisible Doorbell. </p><p>1. Wired Doorbell: This type requires a physical connection to the home's electrical system and typically includes a chime and a button outside the door.</p><p>2. Wireless Visible Doorbell: These doorbells use wireless technology to connect the button outside the door to a visible chime inside the home.</p><p>3. Wireless Invisible Doorbell: These doorbells operate similarly to wireless visible doorbells but have a more discreet design without a visible chime inside the home.</p></p>
