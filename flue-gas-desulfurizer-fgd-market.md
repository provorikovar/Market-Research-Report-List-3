<p><p>Flue Gas Desulfurizer (FGD) market comprises three main types based on the method of removing sulfur dioxide from flue gases. Dry Flue Gas Desulfurizer uses a dry sorbent agent to react with sulfur dioxide. Semi-Dry Flue Gas Desulfurizer combines the features of both dry and wet systems. Wet Flue Gas Desulfurizer uses a wet flue gas scrubbing process with a liquid-sorbent agent to remove sulfur dioxide. Each type has its own advantages and applications depending on the specific requirements of the industry.</p></p>
