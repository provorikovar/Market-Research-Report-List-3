<p><strong>The Conventional Island Deaerator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tray Type Deaerator</li><li>Spray Deaerator</li><li>Membrane deaerator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The conventional island deaerator market consists of three main types: tray type deaerators, spray deaerators, and membrane deaerators. Tray type deaerators utilize trays to separate steam and water, removing dissolved gases efficiently. Spray deaerators use a spray nozzle to disperse water into steam, promoting the release of dissolved gases. Membrane deaerators utilize a hydrophobic membrane to separate dissolved gases from water. Each type offers unique benefits and is suitable for different applications in industries such as power generation and manufacturing.</p></p>
