<p><strong>The Laser Wavelength Meters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through Wavelength Meters</li><li>Absorption Wavelength Meters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wavelength meters can be categorized into two main types: Through Wavelength Meters and Absorption Wavelength Meters. Through Wavelength Meters measure the wavelength of light passing through a medium, while Absorption Wavelength Meters measure the wavelength of light absorbed by a material. Through Wavelength Meters are commonly used in telecommunications and optical networking applications, while Absorption Wavelength Meters are utilized in various industries for analyzing the properties of materials based on their absorption of light at different wavelengths.</p></p>
