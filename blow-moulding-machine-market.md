<p><strong>The Blow Moulding Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fully-Automatic Machine</li><li>Semi-Automatic Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Blow moulding machines can be classified into two main types based on automation level: fully-automatic and semi-automatic machines. Fully-automatic machines require minimal human intervention and operate continuously without the need for manual adjustments. These machines are preferred for high-volume production as they offer greater efficiency and consistency. On the other hand, semi-automatic machines require some manual intervention for tasks such as parison loading and mold closing. They are suitable for smaller production runs or when frequent mold changes are needed.</p></p>
