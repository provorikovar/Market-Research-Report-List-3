<p><p>Charging surge protectors come in various types to accommodate different needs. Voltage switch type surge protectors automatically disconnect power when they detect a surge in voltage. Voltage limited type surge protectors suppress excess voltage to prevent damage. Combination surge protectors offer a mix of features, combining both voltage switch and voltage limited capabilities for comprehensive protection. Each type caters to specific requirements, providing consumers with options to safeguard their devices from power surges.</p></p>
