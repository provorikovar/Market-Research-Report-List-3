<p><p>Water cooling screw chillers are available in two main types - electrodeless adjustment and sectional adjustment. </p><p>- Electrodeless adjustment chillers use advanced technology to automatically adjust cooling capacity based on the cooling load, providing precise and efficient cooling without the need for manual adjustment. </p><p>- Sectional adjustment chillers allow for more flexibility in cooling capacity by dividing the chiller into sections that can be adjusted independently, providing better control over cooling levels in different areas or applications. Both types offer unique benefits and cater to different cooling needs in various industries.</p></p>
