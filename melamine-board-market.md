<p><p>Melamine board market consists of two main types: Melamine Faced Chipboard and Medium Density Fiberboard. Melamine Faced Chipboard is made by compressing wood chips coated with melamine resin, providing a durable and scratch-resistant surface ideal for furniture and cabinetry. Medium Density Fiberboard is made from wood fibers and resin bonded together under heat and pressure, offering a smooth and uniform surface for a variety of applications. Both types are popular choices in the furniture and construction industries for their versatility and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
