<p><p>Booklet makers come in three main market types: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic. Manual booklet makers require the user to manually feed the paper and assemble the booklet. Semi-automatic booklet makers automate some of the processes, such as paper feeding, but still require some manual intervention. Automatic booklet makers automate the entire process, from paper feeding to booklet assembly, making them the most efficient option for high-volume production. Each market type offers varying levels of automation, catering to different production needs and budgets.</p></p>
